Frequesntly Asked Questions

Q: Can I reserve a table?

A: Yes, we accept reservations for groups of 4 or more. Contact us to book!

Q: Do you have Wi-Fi?

A: Absolutely! Brewtopia offers free Wi-Fi to all customers.

Q: Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

A: We provide vegan, gluten-free, and dairy-free options. Let us know your needs!

Q: Do you offer delivery services?

A: Yes, you can order online through our website for delivery or pick-up. We partner with trusted delivery services to ensure your coffee and treats arrive fresh and on time.

Q: Are pets allowed in the cafe?

A: We love furry friends! Pets are welcome in our outdoor seating area. Service animals are allowed inside.

Q: Can I host private events at Brewtopia?

A: Absolutely! Our space is available for private events, from small gatherings to larger celebrations. Contact us for more details and availability.

Q: How can I provide feedback about my experience?

A: We value your feedback! Feel free to use our contact form, email us at feedback@brewtopia.com, or talk to our staff during your visit.

Q: Is parking available?

A: Yes, we have free parking for our customers. Additional street parking is also available nearby.
